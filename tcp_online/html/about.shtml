  • Thomas A.V. Cassel, Professor of Practice and Director, Engineering Entrepreneurship Program
  • Mark Yim, Associate Professor for Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics, Faculty Director for Intergrated Design Master's Program, and Faculty Director of the Weiss Tech House
